Rehabilitation Protocol

Comprehensive 8-week home-based program

Phase 1: Initial Balance (Weeks 2-3)

Fig. 4

Standing Perturbation

Participant stands in bipedal stance. Assistant applies gentle forward perturbation using index/ring finger.

3 Sets 10 Reps 30 sec
Fig. 5

Single Leg Stance (Eyes Open)

Participant stands in a single-leg stance with eyes open, maintaining balance.

3 Sets 10 Reps 20 sec
Fig. 6

Single Leg Stance (Eyes Closed)

Participant stands in a single-leg stance with eyes closed, maintaining balance.

3 Sets 10 Reps 20 sec

Phase 2: Advanced Balance (Weeks 4-9)

Fig. 7

Single Leg Dumbbell Circles

Single-leg stance holding a dumbbell. Perform circular movements with the dumbbell around the trunk.

3 Sets 10 Reps 20 sec
Fig. 8

Partner Palm Touch

Single-leg stance. Attempt to touch partner's palm with hand at varying heights.

3 Sets 10 Reps 20 sec

Cervical Strengthening

Fig. 9

Isometric Wall Press (Occiput)

Place Swiss ball between wall and occiput. Apply gentle isometric pressure backward.

3 Sets 10 Reps 5 sec
Fig. 10

Isometric Wall Press (Forehead)

Place Swiss ball at forehead. Apply forward isometric pressure into the wall.

3 Sets 10 Reps 5 sec
Fig. 11

Ball Press with Resistance Band

Ball at occiput against wall while holding elastic band. Foot anchors band.

3 Sets 10 Reps 30 sec
Fig. 12

Forward Lunges with Ball

Perform forward lunges while holding the Swiss ball overhead.

3 Sets 10 Reps 30 sec
